Our Services

Luxury Appliance Repair You Can Trust.

Luxury Appliance Repair You Can Trust. We don’t service every brand — we specialize only in premium kitchen appliances: Sub-Zero, Viking, and Wolf. That’s why we know them better than anyone.

We repair only premium brands:

  • Refrigerators
    freon leaks, compressors, sensors, control boards, door seals
  • Freezers
  • Wine Coolers
  • Ice Makers
    built-in premium models
  • Outdoor Appliances
    outdoor refrigerators, BBQ fridges, ice makers
  • Wall Ovens
    heating elements, convection systems, sensors, control panels
  • Microwaves
    we do not service other brands
  • Cooktops & Rangetops
    gas, electric, induction
  • Ranges
  • Range Hoods

What we do not service:

  • Dishwashers
  • Washers & dryers
  • Small appliances
    kettles, toasters, etc.
  • Budget brands
    Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, Frigidaire, etc.

While we do not service budget refrigerators, we can still repair ranges, stoves, cooktops, and ovens from most brands — including Samsung, LG, GE, and others.
